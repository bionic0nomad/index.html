❓ FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before booking a demo.

How long does implementation take?
Most teams are live within 5–7 business days. We connect to your existing HRMS or CSV export, configure your compliance rules and org structure, and hand off a working platform — no months-long implementation project required.
Does Daleel integrate with our existing HRMS?
Yes. Daleel supports direct integrations with common HRMS platforms used in Kuwait and GCC. For custom integrations (SAP, Oracle, PeopleSoft, proprietary systems), submit a request and we'll confirm an ETA within 5 business days via our scoping process.
Is the Arabic experience a full translation or a proper RTL interface?
It's a full RTL-native bilingual interface — not a translation layer. Navigation, layouts, forms, reports, and notifications all adjust to Arabic reading direction. Your Arabic-speaking staff get the same experience as English users, not a degraded version of it.
How does CBK compliance monitoring work?
Daleel tracks certification expiry dates, completion rates, and coverage percentages for every CBK-required training (AML, Cyber Security, Banking Awareness, Ethics). You get daily alerts before deadlines, automatic escalations, and one-click scheduling to remediate gaps.
Is our data secure? What's the encryption standard?
AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit. Role-based access control with full audit trails. On-premise deployment available for Enterprise customers who require data residency. Built to the standard that licensed financial institutions in Kuwait expect.
Can we start with one module and expand later?
Yes. Most clients start with Compliance Tracker + Employee Directory, then expand to Learning Intelligence, IDPs, and Analytics as their team gets comfortable. All 22 modules are available — you activate what you need, when you need it.
